Clearing permit application Request (usually from Native Vegetation Conservation Branch) for advice related to a clearing permit application.
Development application Application for planning approval, application to use or develop land, a proposal that does not clearly fall into one of the categories below.
Drain/pump/take water, watercourse works Notice of intent to drain or pump water, licence to take water, permit to undertake works on watercourses.
Extractive industry / mining Application for extractive industry license, mining tenement.
Fenceline / firebreak Applications or referrals related to modifying boundaries (installing/repairing fences, firebreaks, etc).
Greater Bunbury Region Scheme amendment Greater Bunbury Region Scheme amendment or lifting of urban deferred status.
General / pre-referral enquiry Enquiry regarding the planning, use or development of land where the DEC Region has not received a formal request for advice (yet).
Land tenure change Purchase of Crown land, addition to conservation estate, excision, leases.
Management plan / technical report Management plan, technical report, water management plan (excluding subdivision clearance material, which should be input against the relevant subdivision proposal in SRS).
Metropolitan Region Scheme amendment Metropolitan Region Scheme amendment or lifting of urban deferred status.
Pastoral lease permit to diversify Pastoral lease permit required by the Land Administration Act 1997.
Planning scheme / amendment Local planning scheme, district planning scheme, town planning scheme, redevelopment scheme, master plan (e.g. HVWRP master plan) and amendments to these, rezoning, interim development order, special control area.
Policy Policy prepared by State or local government usually, relating to the planning, use or development of land or the environment.
Peel Region Scheme amendment Peel Region Scheme amendment or lifting of urban deferred status.
Structure plan / planning strategy Structure plan, outline development plan, detailed area plan, community layout plan, local planning strategy, regional planning strategy, biodiversity strategy or similar instrument.
Subdivision Subdivision, amalgamation, built strata, survey strata, realignment of boundaries.
Utilities, infrastructure & roads Power, telecommunication, water, pipeline, conveyor belt, waste water, road, railway, airfield, fence line clearing, maintenance, road widening, and includes projects on and off DEC managed areas.
